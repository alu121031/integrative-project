More About Us

This company was founded out of a commitment each and one of us had, to leave this world a better place than how we found it in the sea-life department. We have always been fascinated by this aspect of our world because of all its interesting features and we are excited to see the effect our product will have. Our product is based on the Sustainable Development Goal 14: Life Below Water. Objectives for this goal include:

  • - Target 14.2 Protect and Restore Ecosystems.
  • - Target 14.5 Conserve Coastal and Marine Areas
  • - Target 14.8 Increase Scientific knowledge, reasearch, and technology for ocean health.

The following are some frenquently asked questions.

What are some advantages?
Our product restores and provides marine life a safe habitat where they can flourish and grow up in. Along with this, and increase in biodiversity is likely to happen, as organisms would begin to make their home there.

What are some disadvantages?
Although we hope to benefit the marine environment, there are some disadvantages our product might come with, including: an increase in pollution if done incorrectly, trash can get caught between crevices, damage natural habitats if not properly planned, certain materials could become toxic, end up causing overfishing.

What are the materials used?
We used concrete, plaster, and many more recycled materials.
